About
The Story

Crenshaw, Los Angeles. Self-taught — selling ringtones and mixed CDs in high school before most kids knew what a producer was.

Co-founded HitMob with AceTheFace and RonRon in 2015. Built the blueprint for what the world now calls Traffic Music — the woozy, paranoid, late-night LA sound that defines West Coast rap today.

Breakout: "Vintage & Adventurous" with Conradfrmdaaves. Then Thank You For Using GTL — Drakeo The Ruler rapping through a jail phone, Joog on the other end building history. Pitchfork 8.5. Washington Post. NPR All Things Considered.

2022: debut album Where's Joog? on HitMob. E-40, Earl Sweatshirt, Gunna, 03 Greedo, OHGEESY. West Coast all-star. All on Joog's beats.
