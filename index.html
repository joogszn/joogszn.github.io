About
The Story

Growing up in Crenshaw District, Los Angeles, JoogSzn taught himself music production through the internet — starting by selling ringtones and mixed CDs in high school. Inspired by Quincy Jones, Pharrell, DJ Quik, and Pimp C, he built the woozy, paranoid funk that defines Traffic Music.

In 2015, Joog co-founded HitMob alongside AceTheFace and RonRonTheProducer. His breakout production credit came with "Vintage & Adventurous" by Conradfrmdaaves — a West Coast underground staple that put JoogSzn on the map. He also holds co-production and backend credits on Shoreline Mafia's ShorelineDoThatShit (Atlantic Records), a project that has accumulated over 1 billion US streams.

His defining moment came producing Drakeo The Ruler's Thank You For Using GTL (2020) — recorded entirely over an LA jail phone line. Pitchfork scored it 8.5 and called it the greatest rap album ever recorded from jail. Washington Post, NPR All Things Considered, Passion of the Weiss, and Spectrum Culture all covered it as one of 2020's most important records.

In 2022, Joog stepped from the boards with debut album Where's Joog? on HitMob — 20 tracks featuring E-40, Earl Sweatshirt, Gunna, 03 Greedo, OHGEESY, Mozzy and more.
