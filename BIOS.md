## Long (2000 char max) — Apple Music About, Spotify About, LinkedIn About, BeatStars description

```
JoogSzn is a Platinum-certified producer, engineer, and recording artist out of Leimert Park, Los Angeles.

Self-taught — selling ringtones and mixed CDs in high school before most kids knew what a producer was. In 2015 he co-founded Hit Mob with AceTheFace and RonRon, building the blueprint for what the world would later call Traffic Music — the woozy, paranoid, cinematic sound that shaped a generation of West Coast rap.

The breakout came with "Vintage & Adventurous" for Conradfrmdaaves — a record Gunna remixed and E-40 + Mozzy reworked into "Bay2LA." Then "Thank You For Using GTL" with Drakeo The Ruler — a full album recorded over a jail phone, Drakeo on one end and Joog on the other, building rap history in real time. Pitchfork 8.5. Washington Post. NPR All Things Considered. The most urgent rap album of 2020.

His production runs through Atlantic Records' Shoreline Mafia catalog — RIAA 2x Platinum "Musty," RIAA Gold "Spaceship" (co-prod with RonRon), Gold albums ShorelineDoThatShit and Mafia Bidness (Billboard 200 #27).

In 2022 he dropped his solo debut Where's Joog? on Hit Mob — 20 tracks with E-40, Earl Sweatshirt, Gunna, 03 Greedo, OHGEESY, Mozzy. All-star lineup. All on Joog's beats.

Today he ships music as JoogSzn, beats through BeatStars, and software through Coding Crazy — including SideQuests+ (ADHD productivity RPG) and Pape (Schedule A + split sheet tool for independent artists).

Built for the car ride. Built for the subs. Built to make the ride feel right.

— JoogSzn · Leimert Park · HitMob · Coding Crazy
```
